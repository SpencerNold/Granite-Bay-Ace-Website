About Our Stores
The Granite Bay, Lake of the Pines, Capitol and East Sac ACE Hardware Stores are family-owned and operated businesses that have been serving the greater Sacramento area for many years. Each store has a varied selection of merchandise. CLICK HERE our current ACE Specials.

All stores are known for a great selection of merchandise and a level of service that exceeds any hardware or home improvement store in the region! We are very proud of our stores' reputations and attribute much of it to our friendly and knowledgeable employees.

In addition to a wide range of services, repair parts, and traditional items that you would expect to find in a hardware store, our four stores carry these additional items:
  • Electrical supplies
  • Plumbing supplies
  • Residential and Commercial Hardware Supplies
Our positive and pleasant attitude toward our customers plays a vital role in keeping with our tradition of service excellence. We invite you to visit any of our Ace Hardware locations to find out how we can support you.
